Description of the WFP method

Our cars are autonomous and equipped with a capacity of 1000 liters of water, which is heated to a temperature of 15-20 ° C and goes through 4 stages of cleaning. Thus, warm deionized water is supplied to ultra-light (only 3 kg) carbon rods to a height of up to 25 meters using 100 meter hoses and 12V pumps. Windows, frames and building facades dry streak-free and sparkle with cleanliness. How are facades and windows washed in winter in cold weather?

When washing facades and windows in winter, we add isopropanol (isopropyl alcohol) to the water, which, due to its monoatomic structure (unlike other known ones) evaporates quickly, which is very useful in cleaning at low temperatures. The hardness of isopropyl alcohol is 0ppm so it does not degrade the performance of deionized water.
